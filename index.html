Reasons for changing career path to computer programing and development

Stability

The computer programming and developing career field has a constant demand, and is always growing. The world is becoming more and more reliant on computerts for just about every task. With a few clicks of a mouse or touches on a screen you can do just about anything whether it be ordering food, buying a car, attending a virtual doctor visit, or even submitting school work. With all the reliance on computers for daily task and work to get done, it has created a very stable industry for programers and developers. Stability is the first and most important reason for me to switch over to this career path.

Life Style

The majoprity of jobs in this industry are remote working jobs. The tools required for this job are yourself, a computer, and internet acess. These two things coombined, allow you to have alot freedom. You have the oppertunity to travel significantly more as almost every hotel as wifi acces. You can live in any city and location you want. You won't have to be worrying about commute times, office attire, packing food for your day, or any of the everyday stresses of working from an office.

Income

With the cost of living increasing, it's is very comforting to know that this industry is very competetive with their salaries. A vast majority of entry level positions pay 80k-120k. There is oppertunity for advancement everywhere with all the growth in this industry. A lot companies offer sign on bonuses as well that differ with your expiewrence level.

Options

With all new needs and ideas in this industry, there is almost a never ending amount of different jobs. With being a fullstack energier you can work an almost any area of the project. You would have an endless variety of work to choose from.
